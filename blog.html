About Us

Punjab College for Women (PGC) Bhakkar is a branch of the Punjab Group of Colleges, Pakistan's largest private educational network, established in 1985. It focuses on providing education to women and is known for adopting modern educational tools like ARVO Digital Smart Books. The college is also noted for its commitment to student success through initiatives like merit-based scholarships and talent award ceremonies.
