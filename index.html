Courses We Offer

Here is the list of courses offered by Punjab College Bhakkar.

Matric

Punjab College Bhakkar offers a well-structured Matric (9th & 10th) program designed to prepare students for outstanding success in Board examinations. Every year, our students secure top positions in the BISE board, reflecting the college’s strong academic foundation and disciplined study system.

Intermediate

Punjab College Bhakkar offers a comprehensive Intermediate program (FSc, ICS, ICom, FA) designed to help students achieve exceptional academic success. With a strong history of securing top positions in the BISE Board, our college continues to uphold its reputation for excellence and quality education.

Degree Program

Punjab College Bhakkar offers a well-structured BS/Computer Science (CS) degree program in collaboration with its parent institutions, providing students access to modern computer education and industry-relevant skills. The program is designed to meet the growing demand for IT professionals by combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical learning.

About Us

Punjab College for Women (PGC) Bhakkar is a branch of the Punjab Group of Colleges, Pakistan's largest private educational network, established in 1985. It focuses on providing education to women and is known for adopting modern educational tools like ARVO Digital Smart Books. The college is also noted for its commitment to student success through initiatives like merit-based scholarships and talent award ceremonies.
